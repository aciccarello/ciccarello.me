Here's my collection of hats past and present.
I've developed quite the large collection as it's one of my favorite souvenirs to buy.
Some hats have been retired due to age and ware so I like the idea of keeping a record of them and where I got them.

I'll slowly be adding photos as I take them.
I'm particularly interested in having a bunch of photos of my face but I'm looking to have fun with the locations.

## Retired Hats

### Old Fedora

This gem of an old photo is labeled "Halloween".
Memory of what I was supposed to be escapes me so I'm going to posit I was a young mobster.
I think this was my paternal grandfather's hat that was eventually destroyed by my brother's teething great dane puppy.
RIP.

![Young man eating soup with a button up shirt, fedora, and a napkin tucked into his collar.](/assets/img/hat-fedora.jpg)

### Chicken Hat

This is an iconic piece of personal history.
The chicken hat was originally a halloween costume but became a significant part of my personal identity during my middle school years.
I believe my mother helped me make 4 iterations of this hat.
The first had a wider brim.
One also had a flashlight in the beak.
I'm guessing this is v3.
I still will occasionally have hometown friends or family mention this hat.
It's memory will live on.

![Boy sitting at a picnic table with a custom baseball cap made to look like a chicken with a yellow brim that comes to a point and a red crest.](/assets/img/hat-og-chicken.jpg)
