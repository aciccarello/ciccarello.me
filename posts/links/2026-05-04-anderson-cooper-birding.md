Watching Anderson Cooper reluctantly get into birding is quite comical. He didn't expect to enjoy it. I also love that the vultures were what impressed him because even with beautiful hummingbirds and colorful tropical birds all around, sometimes an all black bird is what brings the drama. Also, shout-out to the Columbian photographer who coolly stated he'd seen 1,600 birds. He seemed so wholesome.
