Before I left New York, I went birding with my sister and brother-in-law at Jamaica Bay.
I'd heard the birding is great there but was really impressed at how much was going on.
I even ID'd 3 lifers!
I couldn't believe how many tree swallows were nesting though.
It seemed like there was another nest box every 50 feet.
