Had lots of fun with the camera at the San Diego Safari Park with my sister.
So many cool animals and fun scenes.
It's not every day you see a man walking an aardvark or a zookeeper named Fern.
I finally caught a (poorly lit) glimpse of the platypus.
I also saw the Sand Cat for the first time (very cute).
Of course I loved the aviaries; one even reminded me of birding in Costa Rica.
