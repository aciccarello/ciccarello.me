Today during the easter service I was reminded of the promise of new life.
Our church has a tradition of sharing flowers which also reminded me of this ranunculus bulb that has flowered in the garden we planted after Bailey died.
The beautiful growth that can come out of pain doesn't take away the pain or justify it, but both can exist and there is something to learn from the contrast.
I'm being challenged to grow now and I hope to see something beautiful from that.
