One of my favorite annual events in oceanside is the [Beach Soccer Championship](https://beachsoccerusa.org/). It's a unique variation of soccer and really fun to watch. The field and teams are small, it is faster pace, you can get close to the action, there are lots of bicycle kicks, and the atmosphere is relaxed like a surf competition. I've now been able to bring a sibling 2 years in a row too.
