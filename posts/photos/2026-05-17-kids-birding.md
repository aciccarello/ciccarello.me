Thanks to my family for coming out this morning.
It was fun sharing the birds with both the kids and adults.
The indigo buntings and orioles were a big hit and it was really cool having the bald eagles to fly over for us.
Even with the big group we [still encountered 32 species](https://ebird.org/checklist/S340169206)!
Also, thanks to my cousin Jude for capturing some photos.
