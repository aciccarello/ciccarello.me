## Itinerary

- Monday
  - Arrived in Varenna via Milan via train
  - Ferry to Menaggio

- Tuesday

  - Photography session
  - Funicolare tram up the hillside to Brunate

  - Ferry was full so had an hour to burn
  - Got back and went to fancy dinner
- Thursday
  - Bellagio
