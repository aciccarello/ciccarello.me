This is a family classic breakfast recipe, great for holidays and birthdays.
Typically we make it the night before and bake it im the morning.

Also checkout the [Breakfast Casserole](/recipes/2022/01/15/breakfast-casserole/) recipe made by the Hoesterey family for a more savory recipe.

1. Grease a glass casserole dish well with a stick of butter
1. Tear up bread and add to the dish
1. Mix 6-8 eggs, a quart of cream, vanilla, and pour over the top
1. Top with cinnamon sugar and diced pieces of the remaining butter
1. Add sliced fruit (blueberries, apples, or strawberries) to the top
1. Bake covered with tin foil at 325F for 30 min and then uncover to brown for 15 minutes
1. Serve with powdered sugar and maple syrup
