This was a christmas gift from my sister and her husband after we asked for an Italian cookbook. It's funny to me now that I didn't recognize the author's face until a couple months later, but Stanley Tucci is a well known actor and played Julia Childâ€™s husband in Julie & Julia. This cookbook is full of personal anecdotes about his family and their cooking traditions. In general, I like reading the commentary of this book even more than making the recipes because it's full of thoughts about what makes meals special. Many of the recipes are more appropriate for a big party than a weeknight dinner, but there are some simpler ones as well. I'd like to check out his other cookbook [The Tucci Table](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/21412297-the-tucci-table) which I think has more staples.
