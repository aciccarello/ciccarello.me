Daily Prayer

Help me pay attention today. I want to be so acutely aware of Your presence in every space. Help me notice beauty that doesn't beg for applause, conversations that carry Your kindness, and the still, small ways You're working beneath the surface. Let my feet be rooted, not rushed. Let my heart be soft, not guarded. And let my soul remember that You waste nothing—not even times of slowing down.
